About DAPO

DAPO (Digital Agri Philippines OPC) is a technology-driven startup dedicated to modernizing the Philippine agriculture sector. Our mission is to digitalize the entire value chain—from production and farm management to distribution and market access.


We aim to empower farmers, livestock producers, and agri-entrepreneurs by providing innovative digital tools that improve efficiency, transparency, and profitability. By bridging the gap between traditional farming and modern technology, DAPO helps build a more resilient and sustainable agricultural ecosystem in the Philippines.

Our Solutions

Livestock Digital Tracking

Monitor livestock health, breeding, and movement using real-time data to improve productivity.

Farm Management System

Digitally manage crops, feeds, inventory, and operations with smart dashboards.

Market Linkage Platform

Connect farmers directly to buyers and suppliers for better income opportunities.

Supply Chain Integration

Ensure efficient, transparent, and traceable farm-to-market processes.
